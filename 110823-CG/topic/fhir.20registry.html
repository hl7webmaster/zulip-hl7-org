Bob Milius (Jan 30 2018 at 22:47):

from Michel Rutten:

The New Orleans Working Group Meeting will be the occasion for the official introduction of http://registry.fhir.org to support the development of FHIR resources. The FHIR Registry is powered by Simplifier.net, and is intended to be the source of truth for FHIR profiles, extensions, terminology-related components and other resources available to the greater FHIR community.

As part of the rollout, a Birds-of-a-Feather session has been scheduled at the conference hotel in the St. James room for Tuesday, January 30, at 7:30pm, following the Corepoint social event, when we should all be in the proper frame of mind to appreciate the benefits the Registry will offer. This session will provide basic introductory training and Q&A to all interested attendees.

I will be available to meet with interested WGs who would like a personal demo or assistance in beginning to use the registry.
