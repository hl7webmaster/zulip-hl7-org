Which are , on your experience pros anc cons of these two standards ?

John Moehrke (Dec 20 2019 at 14:33):

IHE is not a standard. It is a standards organization that focuses only on Implementation Guides (they call them Profiles). IHE takes use-cases and puts forth the best (current) solution leveraging standards, like FHIR. IHE has worked with HL7 (and other standards) side-by-side. IHE has been involved in FHIR from the beginning. IHE thus has many Implementation Guides that use FHIR https://wiki.ihe.net/index.php/Category:FHIR
