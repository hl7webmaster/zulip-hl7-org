Lloyd McKenzie (Sep 15 2019 at 01:43):

HL7 is an organization. HL7 v2, HL7 v3, HL7 CDA and HL7 FHIR are all standards produced by the HL7 organization (along with several other less commonly used specifications).

Lloyd McKenzie (Sep 15 2019 at 01:44):

HL7 standards define how healthcare-related systems can/should share a number of different types of information with other systems.
