Jean Duteau (Apr 12 2019 at 22:33):

Anyone has hl7 ADT41 sample? What does a41 do?

Suman - that is a HL7 V2 message from the Patient Administation domain. You will want to look at Chapter 3 of the HL7 v2 specification. A41 is the trigger event for a Merging of two account records.
