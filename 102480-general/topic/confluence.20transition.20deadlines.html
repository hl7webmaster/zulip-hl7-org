John Moehrke (Oct 30 2018 at 16:53):

What is the current view on expectation for workgroups to move (1) into Confluence, and (2) away from the hl7 wiki?

Joshua Procious (Oct 30 2018 at 17:48):

Hello John, the current expectation for workgroups to move to Confluence is to sign up here: https://confluence.hl7.org/display/RWM/Register+WorkGroup+Migration. During the week specified for the move by the workgroup, I will contact the cochairs and we'll have a screen share to go over specific needs you may have. Futher, I'll set up your space in Confluence and migrate applicable documents. At the time of the workgroup's migration to Confluence, it will be expected that future conference call meetings and workgroup meetings agendas/minutes/attendance will be held on Confluence rather than the wiki. The wiki will continue to exist as an archive. Does that answer your question?
